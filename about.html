A little about me

Hi, I'm Elizabeth Howell, a passionate problem-solver based in Austin, TX. I grew up in a small town near Niceville, FL, where I was lucky enough to spend my time near the stunning beaches of Destin and the peaceful Choctawhatchee Bay.

For the past 11 years, I've thrived as a mechanical engineer in the manufacturing industry, where I specialized in troubleshooting complex systems and improving processes. Now, I’m leveraging my technical background to transition into web development — a field that ignites my creativity and love for problem-solving in new ways.

I graduated from the University of Florida in 2012 with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a Minor in Bio-mechanics. My journey has now led me to build interactive, user-friendly web applications that make an impact.
